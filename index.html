Life (through 26.5 years)

Birth

Born in Miami, FL. Parents met at University of Miami. Mom was a registered nurse and my dad still practices as an ENT doctor.

Growing Up

Attended Pine Crest School for 14 years. PC is an "elite" college prep school. Although tuition was overpriced, I am grateful for my classmates, many of which will be my closest friends for the rest of my life.

College

Attended Lehigh University in Bethlehem, PA to continue my education and play baseball. The Lehigh experience was ok at best, the circle I ran in was heavily influenced by partying and alcohol. However, I did meet my girlfriend there who I plan to spend the rest of my life with!

Early Career

My goal in life was to either be a MLB general manager or a talent agent. Within two years of graduating from college, I worked for a team and agency. I quickly realized my "dream jobs" stifle creativity, suppress young voices, and make it difficult to have the family dynamic that I wish to have.

Next Chapter

In my next chapter, I hope to be pushed outside of my comfort zone with meaningful work that allows me be surrounded by genuinely good people.

Future

If I had to guess, within five years I'll have a family of my own. When the time comes, it is very important for me to have a career that allows me to provide for my family while allowing me the opportunity to be the best father, husband, son, brother possible.
